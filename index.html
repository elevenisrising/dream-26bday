About Dream

The Masked Legend

Clay "Dream" is an American YouTuber and Twitch streamer who revolutionized Minecraft content creation. Known for his incredible speedrunning skills and the iconic Minecraft Manhunt series, Dream became one of the fastest-growing creators in YouTube history.

Beyond Gaming

Dream is also a talented musician, with his debut single "Roadtrip" followed by the viral hit "Mask." His face reveal on October 2, 2022, was watched by millions of fans worldwide, marking a historic moment in internet culture.
